What is a standard size walk-in bath?
A standard size walk-in bath is designed to directly replace your ordinary bathtub, typically in 1500 mm or 1700 mm lengths, with one key upgrade: a low-threshold door.

Safe Access: Low-step entry reduces trip and fall risk.

Familiar Comfort: Full-length size allows traditional lying bathing.

Durable Choices: Choose rugged plastic doors for heavy use or stylish glass doors for modern interiors.
