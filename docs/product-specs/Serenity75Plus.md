Serenity 75 Plus is a spacious and safe bathing solution. The 750 mm width improves freedom of movement, while the 850 mm access opening makes entry and transfer easier.

Its key advantage is safety plus physiotherapy. Hydrotherapy turns everyday bathing into a recovery-friendly routine, while the zero-height threshold minimizes trip risk during entry and exit. This is the package most customers choose when they want a wide bath with practical upgrades.
