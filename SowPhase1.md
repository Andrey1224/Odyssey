1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The objective is to replace the legacy WordPress/Divi website with a modern, high-performance custom website tailored for stability and speed.
Primary Goal: Lead Generation (Phone calls + Enquiry forms).
Design Direction: Clean & modern styling (inspired by Hydrospares), using a proven conversion structure (similar to Practical Bathing).
Strategy: Phase 1 focuses on capturing leads. E-commerce functionality is reserved for Phase 2.
2. PHASE 1 DELIVERABLES
A. Core Pages & Structure
• Homepage: Clear value proposition, trust signals, and strong calls-to-action.
• Product Catalog (Lead Gen Mode): Grid view displaying the 4 core products (No Add to Cart / No Checkout).
• Single Product Page: Gallery, description, specs, and Request a Quote / Call Back buttons.
• About Us: Company story and authority building.
• Contact Us: Contact details, interactive map (if needed), and inquiry form.
• Legal Pages: Privacy Policy / Terms (Client provides text).
B. Content Management (CMS)
• Headless CMS: Sanity.io — admin panel for editing product titles, prices, descriptions, and images without touching code.
C. Technical Stack
• Framework: Next.js (React).
• Mobile-first responsive design.
• Deployment: Vercel (or equivalent) with domain connection.
D. SEO Foundation
• Semantic HTML and performance-first implementation.
• Metadata setup (titles/descriptions).
• Product and LocalBusiness schema markup.
